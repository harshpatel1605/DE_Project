Stress

Work Pressure

Deadlines, long hours, and job insecurity can create significant stress.

Financial Problem

Debt, unexpected expenses, or job loss can lead to financial stress.

Health Issuse

Chronic illness, injuries, or concerns about a loved one's health can be stressful.

Relationship Issues

Conflicts with family, friends, or partners can lead to emotional stress.

Major Life Changes

Events like moving, divorce, losing a loved one, or starting a new job can be stressful.

Lack of Control

Feeling powerless in situations, such as workplace conflicts or societal issues, can cause stress.

Time Management Issues

Feeling overwhelmed by responsibilities and not having enough time to complete tasks.

Social Pressure

Expectations from society, peers, or social media can lead to stress and anxiety.
